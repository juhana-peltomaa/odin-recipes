Banana Pancake

Back to main page

Description

Quick, easy & healthy banana pancake with as little as two ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Two bananas
  • One raw egg
  • Optional: Splash of milk / oatmilk
  • Dash of olive oil for frying (or equivalent)

Equipment:

  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Frying pan
  • Stove

Steps:

  1. Peel the bananas and place them into a bowl
    • Pro-tip: Pre-smush the banana before peeling it
  2. Smush the bananas with a fork in the bowl
  3. Crack egg and add to the bowl with the banana
  4. Mix the above ingredients well. The mixture should start to foam slightly
    • Optional: Add a splash of milk/oatmilk
  5. Warm the stove & frying pan to medium heat and add a dash of olive oil
  6. Add banana/egg mixture to the stove and fry for 5-8 min
  7. Turn once the pancake is getting solid

Enjoy with your selection of toppings! Chef's favourites are:

  • Blueberries
  • Cashews
  • Peanut butter
