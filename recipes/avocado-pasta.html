Avocado Pasta

Back to main page

Description

Hassle free & scrumptious avocado pasta for two!

Ingredients

  • Two ripe avocados
  • 400 grams of spagetti
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • One red chili
  • 0,5 desiliters of olive oil
  • Optional: Lemon, parmesan cheese, fresh basilic, cream

Equipment

  • Bowl
  • Knife
  • Pan
  • Stove

Steps

Spagetti:

  1. Add water to pan and heat until boiling
  2. Add dash of olive oil & boil spagetti for ~8min to be al dente
    • Save 1 desiliter of cooking water

Sauce

  1. Cut chili & garlic into tiny pieces
  2. Open & smush the avocados
  3. Place all of above in a bowl with olive oil & mix!
    • Optional: Add parmesan cheese & lemon juice

Final√©

  1. Add cooked spagetti into the sauce
  2. Add 1 desiliter of cooking water
  3. Mix well & add fresh basilic on top
