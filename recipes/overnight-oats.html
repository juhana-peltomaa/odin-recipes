Overnight Oats

Back to main page

Description

Convenient & healthy breakfast choice from oats!

Ingredients:

  • One desiliter of oats
  • Two desiliters of oatmilk
  • Topping of your choice e.g.
    • Blueberry
    • Apple
    • Banana

Equipment:

  • Bowl or cup
  • Spoon

Steps:

  1. Add oats and oatmilk into bowl. Mix well.
  2. Cut and add your choice of toppings to the bowl
  3. Place bowl in the fridge for 4-8 hours

Pro-tip: Keep the oatmeal in the fridge over night and enjoy it cold for breakfast!
